**On this page**, you will need to introduce your worklog. **How** did you complete it? How many weeks did you take to complete the program? **What** did you do in those weeks?

Typically, and as a standard, a worklog is carried out over about 3 months (throughout the internship period) with weekly contents as follows:

**Week 1:** [AWS Fundamentals: Account Setup, Console & CLI Basics](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [VPC Networking and Hybrid DNS Configuration](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [EC2 Instance Management and S3 Static Website Hosting](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [AWS Storage Services Deep Dive: S3, Glacier, Storage Gateway](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [AWS Security: IAM, Cognito, Organizations, and KMS](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [AWS Database Services: RDS, Aurora, Redshift, and ElastiCache](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Database Migration and Performance Optimization](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Advanced Networking: VPC Design, Security Groups, and Network ACLs](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Serverless Architecture: Lambda, API Gateway, SNS, SQS, and Step Functions](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Monitoring, Cost Management, and Infrastructure as Code](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [AWS Migration Services, Machine Learning, and DevOps Tools](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Final Project, Documentation, and Course Completion](1.12-week12/)
