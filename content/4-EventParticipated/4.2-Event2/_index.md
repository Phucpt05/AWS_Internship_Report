### [Event 1](4.1-Event1/)  
&emsp;**Event Name:** AI-Driven Development using Amazon Q Developer & Kiro  

&emsp;**Date & Time:** 14:00, Friday, October 3, 2025  

&emsp;**Location:** AWS Event Hall, 26th Floor, Bitexco Tower, Ho Chi Minh City  

&emsp;**Role:** Attendee  

&emsp;**Instructors:** Toan Huynh & My Nguyen  

&emsp;**Coordinators:** Diem My, Dai Truong, Dinh Nguyen  

&emsp;**Description:**  
The rise of generative AI marks a transformative shift in software development. This session explored how AI reimagines the entire software development lifecycle, from architecture and development to testing, deployment, and maintenance. With tools like Amazon Q Developer and Kiro, developers can automate heavy lifting tasks and focus on higher-value, creative problem-solving.  

&emsp;**Agenda:**  
- *2:00 PM - 2:15 PM:* Welcoming  
- *2:15 PM - 3:30 PM:* AI-Driven Development Life Cycle overview & Amazon Q Developer demonstration (by Toan Huynh)  
- *3:30 PM - 3:45 PM:* Break  
- *3:45 PM - 4:30 PM:* Kiro demonstration (by My Nguyen)  

&emsp;**Outcomes:**  
Through this event, I gained valuable insights into practical applications of generative AI in modern software development. I learned how AI tools can enhance productivity, reduce repetitive tasks, and support developers in focusing on innovation. This strengthened my understanding of AI’s role in reshaping the industry and inspired me to explore AI-driven development further.
