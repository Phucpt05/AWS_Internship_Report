# Workshop Report "AWS Cloud Mastery Series #1: AI/ML/GenAI on AWS"

### Event Purpose

- Provide comprehensive knowledge about AI/ML services on AWS
- Guide hands-on practice with Amazon SageMaker and Amazon Bedrock
- Share Prompt Engineering and RAG techniques
- Introduce popular Foundation Models and their applications

### Event Information

- **Time:** 8:30 AM – 12:00 PM, Saturday, November 15, 2025
- **Location:** 36th Floor, Bitexco Tower, 02 Hai Trieu Street, Saigon Ward, Ho Chi Minh City

### Key Content

#### 8:30 – 9:00 AM | Welcome & Introduction
- Participant registration and networking

- **Amazon SageMaker**: End-to-end ML platform
